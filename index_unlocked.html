Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga and anime series set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans who devour humans. It follows the story of Eren Yeager and his friends as they fight to survive and uncover the mysteries behind the Titans.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is a popular anime and manga series about a young boy named Tanjiro Kamado who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and save his demon-infested sister.

Death Note

Death Note is a Japanese manga and anime series about a high school student who gains a notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it.

Naruto

Naruto is a popular Japanese anime and manga series about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who seeks recognition and dreams of becoming the strongest ninja in his village, the Hokage.

Pokemon

Pokemon are creatures with various abilities and characteristics that trainers capture and train to battle each other.

One Piece

One Piece is a popular Japanese manga and anime series that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they search for the ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece" in a vast and fantastical world filled with pirates, marines, and mythical creatures.

Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Akira Toriyama, known for its martial arts battles and adventures of Goku, a powerful warrior with a quest for the Dragon Balls, which can grant wishes.

Jiu kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series that follows the story of a high school student who becomes involved in a supernatural battle against curses and evil sorcery.

A boy in modern-day Japan desires to be a mastermind who exerts power from the shadows, but in the process of training, gets hit by a truck and dies.
